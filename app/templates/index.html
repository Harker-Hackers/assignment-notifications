Schoology Calendar Notifications

Why?

Be notified of upcoming assignemnts in your email or discord. With a click of a button, recieve all you need to know about your academic day in a convinient location. As simple as that.

Security

We will never store your passwords. You login using schoology, and we simply check your assignments and send them to your school email. We are open source, so check out our code here, for more.
